Predicting EFTofLSS Counter-terms from Halo Properties

Semester Project with Dr. Arka Banerjee • IISER Pune (Aug 2025 - Present)

Project Overview & Motivation

The Effective Field Theory (EFT) approach for studying LSS models the large-scale universe as an effective fluid including non-linear effects.

$$ \begin{aligned} \left\langle [\tau^{ij}]_{\Lambda} \right\rangle_{\delta_l} &= p_b \delta^{ij} + \rho_b \Bigg[ c_s^2 \delta_l \delta^{ij} - \frac{c_{bv}^2}{Ha} \delta^{ij} \partial_k v_l^k \\ &\quad - \frac{3}{4} \frac{c_{sv}^2}{Ha} \left( \partial^j v_l^i + \partial^i v_l^j - \frac{2}{3} \delta^{ij} \partial_k v_l^k \right) \Bigg] + \Delta \tau^{ij} \end{aligned} $$

Methodology

$$ \tau_{ij} = \rho_m v_i^s v_j^s + \frac{2 \partial_i\phi^s \partial_j\phi^s - \delta_{ij} \partial_k\phi^s \partial_k\phi^s}{8 \pi G} $$
